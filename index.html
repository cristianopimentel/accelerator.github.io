RECORD THE VIDEO

Now you know what you must avoid while recording your video, so it is show time.

This is a PITCH like video, so make it short, 3 minutes maximum, and concise!

TIPS below!

  1. Use a headset/handset - This will make your voice loud, clear and reduce the environment
  2. Look to a light source - recording when you are in front of a light source will increase the video quality
  3. Avoid light source in your back - this will make the camera compensate the light and may distort the video quality
  4. Breath while speaking, use pauses - this will make your speech more clear ang give tension, making the person that is watching to pay more attention;
  5. If you make a mistake, don't stop recording. Edit! - Stopping and starting recording takes a lot of time, keep recording until it is good and then just edit! Use your time smartly;
  6. Avoid reading the scripts - practice so you know what to talk about instead reading. Reading will make your speech look like artificial;
